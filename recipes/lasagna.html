Lisa's homemade Lasagna Recipe

Lisa's homemade lasagna is a classic, delicious dinner that every family should have in their recipe rotation. It's always better homemade!

Ingredient

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • A pinch of Salt and pepper
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2Â¼ cups water
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 lasagne noodles, cooked and drained

Cooking Steps

  1. Brown meat with onions and bell peppers, and season to taste.
  2. Add tomato paste and water till it simmers.
  3. In a mixing bowl, mix ricotta cheese with beaten egg and set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C (370 degrees F)
  5. Butter a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
  6. Begin layering all ingredients beginning with a few spoonfuls of tomato sauce.
  7. Follow with noodles, then ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella.
  8. Repeat until dish is filled.
  9. Bake at 190 degrees C (375 degrees F) about 35 to 45 minutes, until bubbly.
  10. Let cool a couple of minutes before cutting and serving.

Tip: This for a serving for upto seven people
